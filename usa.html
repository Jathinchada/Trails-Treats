Top Food Spots with Amazing Views in the USA

Top of the World Restaurant

Las Vegas, NV

Enjoy a rotating 360-degree view of the Las Vegas skyline from this famous spot in The STRAT tower.

The River Café

Brooklyn, NY

Savor the view of the Manhattan skyline and Brooklyn Bridge from this elegant riverside café.

Sierra Mar

Big Sur, CA

Perched on cliffs above the Pacific, this spot provides breathtaking ocean and mountain views.

Canlis

Seattle, WA

With views of Lake Union and the Cascades, Canlis offers a memorable dining experience.

The Dining Room at the Grand Canyon Lodge

Grand Canyon, AZ

This rustic dining room overlooks the Grand Canyon, providing unforgettable vistas.

The Signature Room at the 95th

Chicago, IL

Located on the 95th floor, this restaurant offers sweeping views of Chicago’s skyline and Lake Michigan.

Aqua by El Gaucho

Seattle, WA

Overlooking Elliott Bay, this waterfront location has views of the Puget Sound and Olympic Mountains.

Chart House Waikiki

Honolulu, HI

This oceanfront restaurant provides amazing sunset views of the Waikiki Marina and Ala Moana Park.

Maison Blanche

Longboat Key, FL

A French-inspired spot with beautiful Gulf views, ideal for a romantic sunset dinner.

Asiate

New York, NY

Located in the Mandarin Oriental, Asiate overlooks Central Park and the New York City skyline.
