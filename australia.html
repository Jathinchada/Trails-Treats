Top Food Spots with Amazing Views in Australia

Quay Restaurant

Sydney, NSW

Enjoy breathtaking views of Sydney Harbour and the Opera House from this iconic fine-dining spot.

Eureka 89

Melbourne, VIC

Located on the 89th floor of the Eureka Tower, this restaurant offers stunning views of Melbourne’s skyline.

Rick Shores

Burleigh Heads, QLD

This beachfront spot offers panoramic ocean views and a relaxed, tropical dining experience.

Skyfeast at Sydney Tower

Sydney, NSW

A revolving restaurant offering a 360-degree view of Sydney, perfect for skyline and harbor views.

Aria

Brisbane, QLD

With views of the Brisbane River and Story Bridge, Aria offers an elegant, scenic dining experience.

Summit Restaurant & Bar

Mount Coot-Tha, QLD

Located on Mount Coot-Tha, this restaurant offers stunning panoramic views of Brisbane and beyond.

Sails on the Bay

Elwood, VIC

This waterfront restaurant provides beachside dining with gorgeous views of Port Phillip Bay.

The Shorehouse

Swanbourne, WA

Enjoy ocean views and sunsets over the Indian Ocean from this relaxed, beachside spot.

C Restaurant

Perth, WA

Perth’s only revolving restaurant, offering 360-degree views of the city and surrounding landscape.

Vue de Monde

Melbourne, VIC

High up in the Rialto Tower, this fine dining restaurant provides incredible views of Melbourne’s skyline.
