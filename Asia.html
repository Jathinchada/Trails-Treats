Top Food Spots with Amazing Views in Asia

Sky Dining at Marina Bay Sands

Singapore

Enjoy panoramic views of Singapore’s skyline and Marina Bay from the iconic rooftop infinity pool.

Rang Mahal

New Delhi, India

A rooftop location with stunning views of the city skyline and an elegant ambiance.

Narisawa

Tokyo, Japan

Located in the heart of Tokyo, Narisawa combines fine dining with views of the bustling Tokyo streets.

Amber

Hong Kong

Located in the Landmark Mandarin Oriental, this spot offers views of Hong Kong’s vibrant skyline.

Vertigo

Bangkok, Thailand

One of Bangkok’s highest rooftop bars, offering spectacular sunset views over the city.

Longtail

Yangon, Myanmar

This riverfront spot provides a relaxing view of the Irrawaddy River and a casual dining experience.

Spago by Wolfgang Puck

Singapore

Located at Marina Bay Sands, this renowned restaurant provides skyline views and an elegant ambiance.

Zafferano

Singapore

This Italian restaurant offers breathtaking views of Marina Bay and Singapore's financial district.

Mikla

Istanbul, Turkey

Overlooking the Bosphorus, Mikla offers a unique dining experience with views of Istanbul’s historic sites.

Sirocco

Bangkok, Thailand

This rooftop restaurant on the 63rd floor offers stunning city views and a memorable dining experience.
