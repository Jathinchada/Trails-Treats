Top Food Spots with Amazing Views in New Zealand & Fiji

Skyline Restaurant

Queenstown, New Zealand

Enjoy stunning views of Lake Wakatipu and the Remarkables mountain range from this mountaintop restaurant.

Peter Gordon's Sugar Club

Auckland, New Zealand

Located at the top of the Sky Tower, this spot offers panoramic views of Auckland's skyline and harbor.

Oceans Restaurant

Paihia, New Zealand

This seaside restaurant offers spectacular views of the Bay of Islands and fresh local seafood.

The Boat Shed

Waiheke Island, New Zealand

A charming coastal dining experience offering views of Waiheke Island’s beautiful beaches and vineyards.

Roko's Restaurant

Nadi, Fiji

Set on the beach, this restaurant provides a relaxing dining experience with views of the South Pacific Ocean.

Tokoriki Island Resort

Mamanuca Islands, Fiji

A stunning spot with breathtaking ocean views, where diners enjoy fresh seafood and Fijian specialties.

Mudbrick Restaurant & Vineyard

Waiheke Island, New Zealand

Located in a vineyard, this spot offers views of Auckland's skyline and picturesque vineyards.

Bamboo Restaurant

Denarau Island, Fiji

Overlooking the ocean, Bamboo serves a fusion of Asian and Fijian dishes with a stunning oceanfront view.

Mt Difficulty Restaurant

Bannockburn, New Zealand

This restaurant offers panoramic views of the vineyards and rugged Central Otago landscape.

Flying Fish Restaurant

Port Denarau, Fiji

Located by the water, Flying Fish offers seafood dishes with views of the Fijian coastline and marina.
